Mumbai's Top 10 Eateries

Discover the best culinary experiences in the city of dreams

From Street Food to Fine Dining

Mumbai, the city that never sleeps, is a paradise for food lovers. From iconic street food stalls to luxurious fine dining restaurants, the city offers a diverse range of culinary delights. We've curated a list of the top 10 eateries in Mumbai that you absolutely must try!

1

The Bombay Canteen

Modern Indian Cuisine

A celebration of India's diverse culinary heritage with a contemporary twist. Chef Thomas Zacharias creates magic with regional Indian ingredients and dishes.

Kamala Mills Compound, Lower Parel
2

Trishna

Seafood

A legendary seafood restaurant known for its butter garlic crab and pomfret tandoori. A must-visit for seafood lovers in Mumbai.

Kala Ghoda, Fort
3

Masala Library by Jiggs Kalra

Progressive Indian

Molecular gastronomy meets traditional Indian cuisine at this innovative restaurant. Famous for their deconstructed dishes and artistic presentations.

BKC, Bandra East
4

Swati Snacks

Gujarati, Street Food

This iconic eatery serves the most authentic Gujarati snacks with a home-style touch. Their panki, sev puri, and dahi batata puri are crowd favorites.

Tardeo, Near Hughes Road
5

Britannia & Co.

Parsi

A heritage restaurant serving authentic Parsi cuisine since 1923. Their legendary berry pulao and sali boti are worth the visit to this vintage eatery.

Ballard Estate, Fort
6

Bademiya

Mughlai, Street Food

A legendary late-night food joint known for its sizzling kebabs and rolls. This street-side eatery has been serving food lovers since 1946.

Behind Taj Mahal Hotel, Colaba
7

Bastian

Seafood, Contemporary

A celebrity hotspot known for its innovative seafood dishes and indulgent desserts. Chef Kelvin Cheung's creations blend global flavors with local ingredients.

Linking Road, Bandra West
8

Cafe Madras

South Indian

An iconic South Indian restaurant serving authentic Udupi and Mangalorean cuisine since 1940. Their filter coffee, idlis, and dosas are legendary.

King's Circle, Matunga East
9

The Table

Global, Farm-to-Table

A fine dining restaurant with a farm-to-table concept, serving globally inspired cuisine. Their small plates and creative cocktails are highlights.

Colaba
10

Pancham Puriwala

North Indian, Street Food

Established in 1865, this is Mumbai's oldest puri bhaji restaurant. Their delicious puris served with potato bhaji and kachoris are a must-try.

Near CST Station, Fort
